Likely

Event Planning Memory Book

Likely is an app that gives small groups the opportunity to plan events, chat, post photos, and assign hilarious superlatives to one another on the go. It's all the best parts of Facebook with the added benefit of not having to actually be on Facebook!

Join different groups, plan multiple events, and you'll be voted Most Likely to have a blast!

Sahara

Amazon Experiment

Sahara is an app that fostered an environment for online shopping experimentation with logins, data management, and a buyer/seller interface. This app takes a look at how a seller would list an item for a buyer to purchase, how to display items for sale with various filters, how a buyer purchases an item, how to update an item's quantity, and how to delete an item.

When Are You Free?

A Dating App for Busy People

The biggest pain in modern dating is matching schedules to actually GO on dates. Of all the dating apps that exist, none prioritize this matching metric. “When Are You Free?” matches users based on their availability and provides a map of places to go based on user location.
